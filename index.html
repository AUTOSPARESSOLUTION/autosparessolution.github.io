About Auto Spares Solution

Established in 2020, Auto Spares Solution is a premier wholesaler of original automobile spare parts for both commercial and private segments. We specialize in providing high-quality components that meet industry standards and ensure optimal vehicle performance.

Our Specializations:

  • Specialist dealers of TVS & STL Fasteners (Nut & Bolt)
  • Authorized distributors of Volume Rubber Products
  • Wide range of OEM and aftermarket parts
  • Bulk supply for workshops and retailers

With our extensive network and industry expertise, we ensure timely delivery of genuine parts at competitive prices. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for automotive businesses across the region.
